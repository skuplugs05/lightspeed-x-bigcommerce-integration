Understanding the Need for Integration

Retailers often rely on a Point of Sale (POS) system like Lightspeed X for in-store operations, while using BigCommerce as their eCommerce platform. While both systems are powerful individually, operating them separately can lead to data silos, manual errors, and inefficiencies.

Without integration, businesses must update product details, stock levels, and orders manually across platforms. This not only consumes valuable time but also increases the risk of overselling, inaccurate inventory, and poor customer experiences.
